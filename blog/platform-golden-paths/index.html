Golden paths for platform teams

Published April 8, 2024

Golden paths are curated workflows that make safe delivery the default. They reduce cognitive load for product teams.

Define the happy path

Capture the common flow for building, deploying, and monitoring services. Make it repeatable and documented.

Provide paved tooling

Offer templates, scripts, and baseline configurations. Reduce the time spent on setup and maintenance.

Embed guardrails

Automatic checks for security, logging, and observability make consistency easy without adding manual work.

Measure adoption

Track how many teams use the golden path. Improvements should be driven by feedback and adoption data.

Golden paths work when they feel faster than the alternatives.
