Performance budgets that actually work

Published February 26, 2024

Performance budgets align teams on what fast means. Without them, dashboards grow and time-to-interactive drifts.

Pick three metrics

Focus on bundle size, load time, and runtime responsiveness. Too many metrics dilute attention.

Automate the check

Use Lighthouse CI or custom scripts to block releases that exceed the budget. Visibility drives behavior.

Break bundles by intent

Lazy-load routes, split feature modules, and prefetch what matters. Treat charts and heavy widgets as optional.

Report on progress

Share budget compliance weekly. Small wins keep the team invested in performance work.

Budgets are a simple contract that keeps user experience front and center.
